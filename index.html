About PlayForge

Where Young Athletes
Find Their Edge

PlayForge Athletics is a dedicated speed and agility training program built specifically for kids ages 6–13. Hosted at J5 Athletics in League City, we combine structured coaching with a fun, encouraging atmosphere.

Whether your child is just discovering sports or already competing on a team, we meet them where they are and help them develop into faster, more coordinated, and more confident athletes.

Structured group and private training sessions
Focused on speed, agility, coordination, and movement
Confidence-building environment for all skill levels

What We Offer

Training That Makes
a Real Difference

Speed Training

Linear acceleration, sprint mechanics, and first-step explosiveness that make a visible difference in every sport your athlete plays.

Agility & Quickness

Ladder work, cone drills, and reactive footwork patterns that build the rapid change-of-direction ability every competitive athlete needs.

Group Sessions

Monday and Tuesday group classes where athletes train together, push each other, and build team-oriented habits that translate to every team they join.

Private Lessons

One-on-one sessions tailored to your child's specific goals. Flexible scheduling and package deals available — contact Coach Rheise directly.

Movement Fundamentals

Body control, balance, and foundational movement patterns that build a platform for athletic growth at every age and skill level.

Confidence Building

An encouraging environment where every athlete is celebrated for their progress. We build the mental edge that separates good athletes from great ones.
